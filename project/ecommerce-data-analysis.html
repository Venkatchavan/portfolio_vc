E-commerce Data Analysis

Comprehensive Analysis of E-commerce Customer Behavior and Sales Patterns

Project Overview

An in-depth analysis of e-commerce data to uncover customer behavior patterns, sales trends, and actionable insights for business optimization. This project leverages advanced data science techniques to transform raw e-commerce data into strategic business intelligence.

Technologies Used

  • Python & Pandas
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn
  • Scikit-learn
  • SQL & PostgreSQL
  • Jupyter Notebooks

Analysis Features

  • Customer segmentation analysis
  • Sales trend forecasting
  • Product performance metrics
  • Churn prediction modeling
  • Revenue optimization insights

Key Insights

  • Customer lifetime value analysis
  • Seasonal purchase patterns
  • Product recommendation engine
  • Marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Inventory optimization recommendations

Business Impact

  • 15% increase in customer retention
  • 20% improvement in conversion rates
  • Data-driven marketing strategies
  • Optimized inventory management
  • Enhanced customer experience

Results & Impact

The analysis revealed critical insights into customer behavior, leading to strategic improvements in marketing campaigns, inventory management, and customer retention strategies. The data-driven approach resulted in measurable business improvements and enhanced decision-making capabilities.
